Help

How do I install Flash?

####What are the system requirements?
You can use our HTML5 player or Flash player to watch on PCs running Windows 7+, and Intel-based Macs running OSX 10.7+ (Lion). You will need a broadband or fibre internet connection - dial up will not work. You’ll find a complete list of requirements below.

####Supported browsers for HTML5 player
Google Chrome on Windows 7+ and Mac OSX 10.7+ (Lion).
Firefox on Windows 7+ and Mac OSX 10.7+ (Lion).
Opera on Windows 7+ and Mac OSX 10.7+ (Lion).
Edge on Windows 10.
Internet Explorer 11 on Windows 8+.
We recommend that you update to the latest version of your browser.

####Supported browsers for Flash player with Widevine plugin
Internet Explorer 10+ on Windows 7.
Safari on Mac OSX 10.7+ (Lion).
You may be prompted to install the Flash Player and Widevine plugins.
We recommend that you update to the latest version of your browser.

####Can I watch content on Windows 10?
Yes, you can watch content on Windows 10 using Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Edge or Internet Explorer.

####I can’t watch any of the films that I have bought, what’s going on?
Check your minimum system requirements above.
We recommend that you update to the latest version of your browser.
If you are using a browser that uses the Flash player, check that you have the Widevine plugin installed here and that you have the latest flash player installed here.
We use adaptive bitrate streaming which means the streaming quality will automatically adjust based on your internet speed. For High Definition (HD) streaming we recommend VDSL, cable or fibre connections as we cannot guarantee consistent HD streaming on ADSL.

####Are the film available in High Definition (HD)?
Yes. For High Definition (HD) streaming we recommend VDSL, cable or fibre connections as we cannot guarantee consistent HD streaming on ADSL.

####What is Widevine?
Widevine is a plug-in for your web browser that is used to play our video content. Widevine is a free add-on for your browser that is owned by Google. If you do not already have this installed when you attempt to play a video title in your web browser you will see a notification that will direct you to the Widevine website, from there you simply need to click on Install Now and follow the on screen prompts. If you want to install Widevine now just click this link.

####How do I install Widevine?
The Widevine installation varies depending on the browser you are using and is only required for Safari and some versions of Internet Explorer. Here are the detailed installation instructions for Internet Explorer 11.

####How do I install Flash?

The Flash installation varies depending on the browser you are using and is only required for Safari and some versions of Internet Explorer. Here are the detailed installation instructions for Internet Explorer 11.

####What happens if I forget my password?
Simply click Forgot your password? on the Sign In page, enter the email address you used when you created an account and a link to reset your password will be sent to you.

####Why does playback of my film stall or not play smoothly?
If your internet connection is poor or inconsistent you may experience problems during playback.

####Can I watch content on my iPhone/iPad?
Yes, you can watch content using the SHIFT72 app which can be downloaded from the App Store. The app requires iOS 8 or later and allows you to stream content. Download along with the ability to watch offline is currently in development.

####Can I watch content on my Android Device?
Yes, you can watch content using the SHIFT72 app which can be downloaded from the Google Play Store. The app requires Android 4.1 or later and allows you to stream content and download to view offline.

####Can I watch content on Linux?
Yes, you should be able to using Chrome on Linux, but this is not something we actively support.

####What devices can I Chromecast from?
You can Chromecast from a Chrome web browser or from an Android device using the SHIFT72 app.

####How do I watch content using Chromecast from Chrome?
Make sure you have connected your Chromecast to your TV and Wi-Fi network then follow the steps below. Your computer must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your Chromecast. For information or assistance connecting Chromecast to your TV and/or Wi-FI network, see support.google.com/chromecast.
1. Make sure you’re using the latest version of Chrome by navigating to Settings > About Google Chrome and update your Chrome browser to the latest version, if necessary. If you’re having trouble, here’s how to update Chrome.
2. Download the Google Cast extension.
3. Once installed, in the top right corner of your Chrome browser, you should see the Cast button.
4. In Chrome browser go to the film or TV page and start playing the content.
5. Select the Cast icon in the bottom right hand corner.
6. Select your Chromecast device.
7. The film or TV episode will now start playing on your Chromecast device.
